Purpose

Everyone knows that actions speak louder than words. Why tell you what I can offer your company when I can show you. Here you will see a visual representation of the types of skills and creativity I have for web design. Take a look around, engage with the content, and visualize the kind of solutions I can bring to your next project.

Audience

It's been quite the journey to get to where I am today. After many internship rejections I decided I was tired of trying to verbally convince potential employers that I was a capable web designer. Hence the reason for this digital portfolio. I figured nothing can adequately get my point across like visual evidence of my claims. So this is for you potential employers. I have created this digital portfolio to wow you with my skills, and so you no longer have to take my word for it.

Content

Home page

I have created this digital portfolio as an opportunity to showcase my skills in Web Designing and Implementation. A passion of mine is creating interactive websites that allow the user to explore site content the way they want to. Sprinkled throughout my digital portfolio you will find hyperlinks leading the user to subpages with more in-depth information, along with images specifically selected to enhance the information being presented on each page. Besides information the user should also get a feel for the websites tone based on the color pallete chosen, and the fonts used. Every aspect of a websites creation matters as it adds to the overall vibe and website aesthetic. You want to give the user the opportunity to interact with the website the way they want to as a client or consumer, while evoking certain feelings from them through the use of palletes, images, and overall layout.
