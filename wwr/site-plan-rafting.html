Purpose

The purpose of creating this website is to attract more customers to our white water rafting company. We are working on our social media prescence to reach more customers across more platforms. Hopefully this website will create more foot-traffic and therefore more revenue.

Audience

Target audience is families looking for activities to do in the area while on their vacation. When searching on their computers what to do in the area with kids, we hope our wesbite will be picked up by the search engine, and they will viist our website, read about us, see our prices, view our images and testimonials, and decide to visit.
